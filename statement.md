## Problem Statement
  We often experience moments of mental fog, yet most of us never track when or why it happens. Without a simple system to record these changes, it’s difficult to      notice patterns or improve our cognitive habits.  The Cognitive Fog Analyzer addresses this problem by providing a quick, user-friendly way to measure and store      daily mental clarity levels.

## Scope of the Project 
  - Let users quickly record how clear or foggy they feel each day.
  -Calculate and show a simple fog score.
  - Save daily entries for future reflection.

## Target Users
-Students who want to track their focus and study clarity.
-Working professionals dealing with stress or long work hours.
-Individuals experiencing mental fatigue or brain fog.
-People who want to build self-awareness about their daily mental state.
-Anyone looking for a simple tool to track clarity without medical complexity.

## High level features
-Daily input sliders for focus, sleepiness, understanding, and productivity.
-Automatic fog score calculation.
-Instant feedback on fog level (Low, Mild, High, Severe).
-CSV-based storage for saving daily entries.
-Simple, clean, and easy-to-use Tkinter interface.
-Ability to review past records for pattern tracking.
